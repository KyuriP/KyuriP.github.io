D.I.Y. dog clothes

First attempt to make clothes for my dogs.

Recently I cleaned out my closet and found a bunch I wouldn’t wear. Then, I thought: “why don’t I try making clothes for my dogs?” I instantly looked up D.I.Y. dog clothes on the Internet, and followed the Wikihow “Easy and Fun DIY Dog Outfts” Well…. Now that I look back, I was being silly. It was so much more difficult than it seemed. Although the clothes didn’t turn out pretty, my dogs didn’t seem to hate them. Maybe I will give it another go some day!
